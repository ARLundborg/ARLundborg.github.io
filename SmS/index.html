Statistical Methods for SCIENCE

Course homepage – block 2, 2023/2024

This is the homepage of the above PhD course given by the Data Science Laboratory hosted by the Department of Mathematical Sciences and the Department of Computer Science. This homepage contains information and material such as course plans, slides, exercises and R files. The lecturer is Anton Rask Lundborg with TA Ulises Bercovich Szulmajster assisting at the exercises.

Course information and material

  • Credits: 4.5 ECTS.
  • Software: R, RStudio. R is free and open source and the RStudio IDE is free to use. You may learn more and download the programs from the links.
  • Book: Martinussen, Skovgaard, Sørensen, “A first guide to statistical computations in R”, Biofolia 2012, ISBN 978-87-913-1956-3. The book is available from Academic books on Frederiksberg Campus. Your old textbook on introductory statistics (whatever that is) might also be useful.
  • Additional and optional R exercises that may help you to learn or refresh R: R exercises
